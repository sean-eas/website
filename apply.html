Trustee Opportunity – Elijah’s Animal Sanctuary

About Elijah’s Animal Sanctuary

Elijah’s Animal Sanctuary is a small, values-led charity providing lifelong sanctuary care for animals who cannot be rehomed. Every animal in our care is offered safety, dignity and compassion for the rest of their lives.

The sanctuary was founded in 2022 by Sean and Becca, named after their Son Elijah who was sadly born sleeping in 2019. The sanctuary is Elijah’s legacy and is rooted in kindness, integrity and responsible stewardship. As we continue to grow, we are focused on building strong foundations that ensure the charity remains sustainable, ethical and well-governed for the long term.

The Role

We are looking to appoint a new Trustee to join our Board and help guide the future of Elijah’s Animal Sanctuary.

Trustees are volunteers who work collectively to ensure the charity is well run, financially sustainable and always acting in its best interest.

This is not a day-to-day operational or hands-on animal care role.

Minimum Time Commitment

  • Four trustee meetings per year, each lasting approximately 2 hours
  • Around 4 hours per month supporting the charity outside of meetings

Meetings are primarily held in person in Mirfield, West Yorkshire so being local or willing to travel to Mirfield is essential.

Who We’re Looking For

  • Genuine passion for animal welfare
  • Experience working with charities in some capacity (trustee, volunteer, employee, advisor, or consultant)
  • Share our values and understand the importance of lifelong sanctuary care
  • Thoughtful, collaborative and comfortable contributing to strategic discussions
  • Willing to take on the legal responsibilities of a trustee

You do not need prior trustee experience. We care more about integrity, judgement and a willingness to learn.

Eligibility

  • Be aged 18 or over
  • Be eligible to act as a charity trustee under Charity Commission rules
  • Be willing to complete a trustee eligibility declaration
  • Be UK based

How to Apply

To apply, please email apply@elijahsanimalsanctuary.org with:

  • Your CV
  • Answers to the questions below (in the body of the email or as an attached document)

If we have sufficent applications by 1700 Sunday 25th January 2026 we will close the position. If we do not we will extend this deadline by a week. We aim to respond to all applicants by Sunday 1st February

Application Questions

  1. Why are you interested in becoming a trustee of Elijah’s Animal Sanctuary? (Max 250 words)
  2. Please tell us about any experience you have working with charities. (Max 250 words)
  3. What skills, knowledge, or experience do you feel you could bring to the trustee team? (Max 500 words)
  4. What do you understand the role of a charity trustee to be? (Max 250 words)
  5. Can you give an example of a time you have had to contribute to, or help make, a difficult or sensitive decision? (Max 250 words)
  6. What does animal welfare mean to you, particularly in the context of lifelong sanctuary care? (Max 250 words)

Our Commitment

Elijah’s Animal Sanctuary is committed to fairness, transparency and inclusion. We welcome applications from people of all backgrounds and will always treat applicants with respect and care.

If you are motivated by compassion, responsibility and the desire to make a lasting difference for animals who need sanctuary, we would love to hear from you.
