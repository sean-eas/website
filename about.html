About Us

Elijah's Animal Sanctuary is a volunteer-run safe haven for animals who cannot be rehomed due to age, health, or behavioural issues. Named after our founders’ son, Elijah, who was sadly born sleeping, the sanctuary is his legacy — a space filled with compassion and care.
